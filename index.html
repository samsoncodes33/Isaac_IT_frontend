WELCOME TO SIFMS

Student Information & Feedback Management System

The Student Information & Feedback Management System (SIFMS) of the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN) is designed to enhance transparent communication between students and the Student Union Government (SUG), especially the Director of Information (DOI).

Students can submit complaints, feedback, and suggestions directly to the DOI for prompt attention and resolution.

About SIFMS
